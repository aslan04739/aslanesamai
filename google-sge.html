Speed

AI-generated results are slow. On some queries, I had to wait, on average, 8 seconds for the results to show up :)

Choice

Users get to decide what kind of results they want: with some queries, you’ll get a “Generate” button that will give you AI results if you click on it.

Featured snippets

Good’ol featured snippets still show up. I even got them with AI-generated results.

Content attribution

Whether we like it or not, Google does not mention the websites it used to fetch the content. Which is bad for content creators.

Content freshness

I searched for the latest Macbook Air launched by Apple. The AI-generated results mention the M2 13 inch Macbook AIR. In reality, the latest Macbook Air is the 15 inch edition they unveiled at WWDC. This means AI-generated results will not always provide the latest information.

Weirdly though, I asked for the latest products Apple launched at WWDC, and the 15-inch MacBook Air was mentioned.
