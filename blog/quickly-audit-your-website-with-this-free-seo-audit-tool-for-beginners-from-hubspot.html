When you think of HubSpot, two things come to mind: Inbound marketing strategies and their remarkable CRM platform. However, there's an aspect of HubSpot that often flies under the radar but holds immense value, especially for SEO beginners: their ingenious SEO tool.

Yes, you heard it right. HubSpot offers a straightforward, user-friendly, and completely free site grader that's tailored perfectly for those who are just starting their journey into the world of Search Engine Optimization (SEO).

So, what's the scoop on this nifty tool? Let's dive in:

A Quick SEO Audit Made Simple

The HubSpot Site Grader isn't just another tool in the sea of SEO offerings. It stands out for its simplicity and ease of use, making it an ideal starting point for SEO beginners who want a hassle-free audit.

Here's a glimpse of what this tool brings to the table:

1. Performance Review

The Site Grader takes a thorough look at the performance aspects of your website. From analyzing page speed and size to evaluating the number of requests, caching effectiveness, and redirects, it leaves no stone unturned. What's more, it then compares your website's results to industry benchmarks, providing you with a score that encapsulates your website's performance.

2. SEO Check

For those of you who are just beginning to explore the world of SEO, the Site Grader proves to be an invaluable companion. It inspects the fundamental elements of an SEO-optimized page. This includes assessing descriptive meta descriptions, anchoring texts for clarity, and even verifying the presence of crucial index tags. These elements, when combined, contribute significantly to enhancing your website's visibility on search engines.

It's worth noting that while the Site Grader's Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) might not be the most intricate, they're intentionally designed with SEO beginners in mind. The focus is on providing you with a solid foundation for understanding the essentials of SEO.

3. Mobile Usability

In today's mobile-centric digital landscape, mobile usability is paramount. The Site Grader recognizes this and ensures that your website is not only legible on mobile devices but also responsive. These factors play a pivotal role in delivering a seamless user experience across different screen sizes.

4. Security Check

No SEO audit is complete without addressing security concerns. The Site Grader steps up here too, verifying whether your website serves pages over HTTPS, which is crucial for establishing trust with your users. Moreover, it takes a close look at your JavaScript libraries, ensuring that they are secure and free from vulnerabilities.
