SEO Case Study Overview

Sometimes, SEO success is a couple of tweaks away

This SEO case study focuses on Al Fadjr, a newspaper site dedicated to delivering news to its French-speaking audience. With an underperforming website and several SEO challenges, the company sought to improve its online visibility and attract the right audience. Despite time constraints and limited team support, we implemented a targeted strategy that included keyword research, backlink analysis, content creation, technical improvements, and more. We improved clicks by 90% and impressions significantly by focusing on just two key areas. Sometimes, SEO success is just a couple of tweaks away. This case study highlights the step-by-step approach, challenges faced, and the remarkable results achieved, showcasing the power of a well-crafted SEO strategy. Let's dive into how Al Fadjr defied expectations and achieved remarkable growth through strategic SEO initiatives.

SEO Problem

Al Fadjr faced numerous SEO issues:

  • Indexing Issues: News sites often have a large volume of content, leading to challenges in ensuring timely and relevant articles are indexed.
  • Duplicate Content: Frequent updates and similar articles can lead to duplicate content issues, impacting SEO performance.
  • Content Freshness: Ensuring that content remains fresh and relevant is crucial, but difficult, especially with evergreen topics.
  • High Bounce Rates: News sites often experience high bounce rates due to users coming for specific articles and leaving immediately after.
  • Slow Page Load Times: Rich media and ads can significantly slow down page load times, negatively impacting user experience and SEO.
  • Mobile Optimization: With a significant portion of users accessing news sites via mobile devices, ensuring a seamless mobile experience is critical.

Additionally, Al Fadjr encountered common SEO problems that news sites usually face:

  • The majority of pages were not indexed Site was deemed as not secure by Google Lack of internal linking on pages. Low site health overall. No backlinks to the website. No irrelevant meta descriptions on pages. Absence of H1 titles on pages.

The SEO Strategy

Setting Up SSL Certificate

One of the first and most crucial steps we took was setting up an SSL certificate for Al Fadjr. This converted all HTTP pages to HTTPS, significantly improving the site's security and accessibility. Previously, users received notifications from Google indicating that the site was not secure, which deterred many readers. With the SSL certificate in place, these warnings disappeared, and the site became fully accessible to readers.

Creating a 200 OK Sitemap

Given the size of the site, creating a comprehensive sitemap was essential. Using Screaming Frog, we generated a 200 OK sitemap. This process was time-consuming due to the extensive number of pages, but it was necessary to ensure that Google could crawl and index the site efficiently.

Avoiding Index Bloat

Al Fadjr had a lot of quality content, but much of it was irrelevant due to the nature of a news site where timely content is key. Initially, we manually controlled the indexing process, ensuring only the most important pages were indexed. Once all pages were converted to HTTPS, Google started to trust the site more.

Additional SEO Recommendations
  1. Implementing Lazy Load: The first element users saw was a heavy slideshow. I recommended implementing lazy loading to improve site speed and user experience.
  2. Using Consequential Headings: The team was using H1, H2, etc., randomly. I advised them on proper usage to enhance SEO.
  3. Working on Google Discovery: To increase visibility on Google's Discover feature.
  4. Improving Mobile Friendliness: Ensuring the site was optimised for mobile users.
  5. Separating Videos from Written Content: Following Google's guidelines on video content.
  6. Adding Structured Data: Opportunities to add structured data for organizations, blogs, authors, etc.
  7. Improving URL Structures: Suggested more descriptive URL structures for better SEO.
  8. Adding Meta Descriptions: Recommended adding relevant meta descriptions to pages.
  9. Addressing Thin Content: Suggested setting a word count minimum for future content to avoid thin content issues.
  10. Internal Linking: Educated the team on the importance of internal linking and helped implement it.
  11. Backlink Acquisition: Introduced the concept of backlink building and suggested opportunities.
