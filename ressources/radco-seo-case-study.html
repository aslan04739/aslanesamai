The SEO problem

RADCO faced significant challenges with its online visibility and website performance. The issues included:

  • Limited online visibility beyond the Google Business profile.
  • - Numerous 404 errors on the website.
  • - Articles lacking structure and content.
  • Lack of a sitemap or robots.txt.
  • Unconfigured Google Analytics and Google Search Console.
  • Lack of awareness about competitors' SEO strategies.
  • Presence of irrelevant pages and template pages left behind.
  • Important pages like projects, services, and about us not indexed.
  • Absence of internal linking and relevant meta descriptions.
  • No H1 titles on pages.
  • Images lacking alt text and not served as next-gen pictures (webp).
  • Overall low site health.
  • Absence of backlinks.

These challenges presented a comprehensive set of issues that needed to be addressed to improve RADCO's online presence and performance.

The Strategy

Technical SEO:

  1. Optimizing and Adding Meta Descriptions: Prioritized starting with pillar pages to streamline the time-consuming task of adding meta descriptions.
  2. Submitting a Sitemap on GSC: Implemented a sitemap submission on Google Search Console (GSC) to ensure better crawling and indexing.
  3. Configuring GSC and Google Analytics: Recognized the value of reliable data from GSC for free, and configured both tools for improved analytics.
  4. Addressing Crawled but Unindexed Pages: Manually submitted crucial pages like Home, About Us, and Pricing to Google Search Console.
  5. WordPress and Plugin Updates: Ensured the WordPress version and plugins were up-to-date, and removed unused and conflicting plugins.

Off-page SEO:

Backlinks and Company Directories: Acknowledged the importance of backlinks for visibility in a competitive field. Explored company directories as an alternative in a region where backlinks were not yet prevalent.

On-page SEO:

  1. Structured Data Implementation: Introduced structured data, including organization, article, and services, to enhance search engine understanding.
  2. Content Creation: Recognizing the importance of humanizing the company, created blog articles around targeted keywords to engage with the audience.
  3. Social Media Integration: Linked to the company's LinkedIn profile to further connect with the audience.
  4. Image Optimization: Converted images to webp format for improved website speed.

Keyword Research:

Conducted keyword research using tools such as SEMrush, and Ubersuggest.

  1. Competitor Analysis: Identified the competitors.
  2. Keyword Gap Analysis: Conducted analysis to understand competitors' keyword strategies and identified relevant keywords.
  3. Keyword Proposal: Proposed relevant keywords to the team based on low keyword difficulty and average search volume.

Results

After six months of implementing the SEO strategy:

  1. Improved Site Speed: The website is noticeably faster.
  2. Enhanced Visibility: The site ranks first for branded queries.
  3. Google Recognition: Google acknowledges the website on Google Maps, using a description from the website.
  4. Keyword Traction: The website is gaining traction for targeted keywords.
