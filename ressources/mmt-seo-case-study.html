This SEO case study focuses on MMT, a mobile medical team company targeting people in Riyadh, KSA. With a website that was nearly invisible in search results, numerous technical SEO issues, and stiff competition, the company sought to enhance its online visibility and attract the right audience.

We implemented a comprehensive strategy that included keyword research, backlink analysis, content creation, technical improvements, and more. This case study highlights our step-by-step approach, the challenges we faced, and the outstanding results achieved, showcasing the power of a well-crafted SEO strategy. Join us as we dive into how MMT surpassed expectations and achieved significant growth through strategic SEO initiatives.

The SEO Problem

MMT faced significant challenges with its online visibility and website performance. The issues included:

  • No Google Search Console or Google Analytics setup, leading to unsubmitted sitemaps and unindexed pages.
  • The website was targeted by a malicious network of toxic backlinks.
  • WordPress and Yoast were serving archive and dated pages, resulting in near duplicates.
  • WordPress was serving RSS feeds by default, causing no-index tags on pages in Google Search Console.
  • Non-descriptive Arabic URLs leading to unoptimized SEO performance.
  • Numerous test pages left undeleted, resulting in 404 and 301 pages.
  • Multiple H1 titles and meta-descriptions across pages.
  • Presence of "Lorem ipsum" content.
  • No structured data detected.
  • Images served in jpg and png without alt text, slowing down the site.
  • Orphan pages detected.
  • Issues with site structure (404 pages, orphan pages, non-consequential pages).
  • Lack of exciting traffic.
  • Missing X default Href lang for Arabic.
  • Numerous irrelevant template pages left behind.
  • Absence of internal linking.
  • No backlinks.
  • No keyword strategy.

The Strategy

Keywords

My first step was finding the appropriate keywords for MMT. I started by doing some research on Google and found a few competitors:

  1. Medical Care4all
  2. Health Dar
  3. Eshfaa
  4. Care Med

Once I wrapped up the keyword gap analysis, I exported the keyword data into a CSV file and did some number-crunching using SQL. Since there weren't many keywords to work with, I aimed for the high-relevance, high-difficulty ones to fine-tune our targeting efforts and boost our SEO game.

Technical SEO

  1. Optimizing and Adding Meta Descriptions: Started with pillar pages to streamline adding meta descriptions.
  2. Submitting a Sitemap on GSC: Implemented a sitemap submission on Google Search Console for better crawling and indexing.
  3. Configuring GSC and Google Analytics: Configured both tools for improved analytics and reliable data.
  4. Addressing Crawled but Unindexed Pages: Manually submitted crucial pages like Home, About Us, and Pricing to Google Search Console.
  5. WordPress and Plugin Updates: Ensured the WordPress version and plugins were up-to-date, removing unused and conflicting plugins.

Off-page SEO

Backlinks

We recognized the importance of cleaning up our backlink profile, so we disallowed toxic backlinks using Google's Disavow Tool. This proactive step ensured these harmful links no longer negatively impacted our site's reputation and performance.

On-page SEO

Content Creation

For content creation, we relied on two aspects:

  1. Google Search Console data: After setting up Google Search Console and giving it time to collect data, we analyzed what was performing well and what wasn't.
  2. Content Gap Analysis: We already had focus keywords and started publishing content once a week, monitoring the results.

Technical Tweaks

  • Changed the LAN attribute to FR on all pages.
  • Used Google Search Console to remove irrelevant pages, fix 404s, and delete unnecessary template pages.
  • Manually submitted important pages to Google Search Console, considering its daily limit.
  • Added canonical tags on duplicate pages and fixed internal links.
  • Addressed indexation issues for orphan pages by using GSC to add them to the sitemap.
  • Optimized site speed by converting images to WebP format and using alt texts.

Results

Our hard work and strategic approach paid off, resulting in:

  • 100% increase in organic traffic within six months.
  • 75% improvement in average session duration.
  • 50% reduction in bounce rate.
  • 200% increase in the number of indexed pages.
  • Significant improvements in keyword rankings, with many targeted keywords reaching the first page of search results.

MMT's SEO transformation showcases the power of a well-executed strategy. By addressing technical issues, implementing a robust content plan, and continuously monitoring and adjusting our approach, we were able to achieve remarkable results and drive significant growth for MMT.
