Key Challenges

When we first audited the client's website, we discovered a range of critical issues hindering its performance:

Our initial audit revealed several critical issues:

  • Lack of Google Search Console and Google Analytics: Without these essential tools, the site map hadn't been submitted, meaning Google was unaware of the site's existence. Consequently, most pages were not indexed.
  • Toxic Backlinks: The site was targeted by a malicious network, impacting its credibility and ranking.
  • WordPress and Yoast Configuration Issues: Archive and dated pages were being served as near duplicates. Additionally, RSS feeds were incorrectly set to no-index in Google Search Console.
  • Multilingual SEO Challenges: The site offered content in both English and Arabic, but the Arabic URLs were non-descriptive, leading to poor SEO performance.
  • Developer Oversights: Numerous test pages were left live, resulting in a plethora of 404 and 301 errors.
  • Improper Use of Headers and Meta Descriptions: Pages across the site had multiple H1 titles and meta descriptions, confusing search engines.
  • Placeholder Content: "Lorem ipsum" filler text was found, reducing the site's professionalism.
  • Lack of Structured Data: No structured data was detected, missing out on enhanced search listings.
  • Image Optimization Issues: Images were served in JPG and PNG formats without alt text, slowing down the site.
  • Orphan Pages: Pages without any internal links were found, making them hard to discover by users and search engines.
  • Site Structure Problems: The site had numerous issues, including 404 errors, orphan pages, and non-consequential pages, leading to poor navigation.
  • Unexciting Traffic: The site failed to attract and retain visitors due to its various issues.
  • Missing Href Lang Attributes: Arabic pages lacked the X-default Href Lang tag, affecting multilingual SEO.
  • Irrelevant Pages: Template and irrelevant pages were left behind, cluttering the site.
  • Absence of Internal Linking: The site had no internal linking, which is crucial for SEO and user experience.
  • No Backlinks: The site lacked backlinks, essential for building domain authority.
