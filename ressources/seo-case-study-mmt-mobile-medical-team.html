SEO Case Study Overview

At MMT, a Mobile Medical Team company based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, we took on the exciting challenge of helping a new startup find its place in a competitive market. The site had numerous technical SEO issues, and the competition was fierce. But with a strategic, heartfelt approach, we turned these challenges into opportunities. This case study is not just a story of technical achievements but a testament to how dedication, expertise, and a personal touch can transform a brand's online presence, making MMT a trusted name in their field.

Established websites face the daunting task of staying ahead in a constantly evolving digital landscape:

Key Challenges

When we first audited the client's website, we discovered a range of critical issues hindering its performance:

Our initial audit revealed several critical issues:

  • Lack of Google Search Console and Google Analytics: Without these essential tools, the site map hadn't been submitted, meaning Google was unaware of the site's existence. Consequently, most pages were not indexed.
  • Toxic Backlinks: The site was targeted by a malicious network, impacting its credibility and ranking.
  • WordPress and Yoast Configuration Issues: Archive and dated pages were being served as near duplicates. Additionally, RSS feeds were incorrectly set to no-index in Google Search Console.
  • Multilingual SEO Challenges: The site offered content in both English and Arabic, but the Arabic URLs were non-descriptive, leading to poor SEO performance.
  • Developer Oversights: Numerous test pages were left live, resulting in a plethora of 404 and 301 errors.
  • Improper Use of Headers and Meta Descriptions: Pages across the site had multiple H1 titles and meta descriptions, confusing search engines.
  • Placeholder Content: "Lorem ipsum" filler text was found, reducing the site's professionalism.
  • Lack of Structured Data: The absence of structured data made it difficult for search engines to understand the site's content and services.

Strategic SEO Solutions Implemented

To tackle these challenges, we implemented a comprehensive strategy:

  • Site Health Checks: Conducted in-depth site audits using tools like Google Search Console and Google Analytics to track site health and performance.
  • Disavowed Toxic Backlinks: Cleaned up the backlink profile by disavowing toxic links.
  • Optimized WordPress Configuration: Adjusted Yoast settings to eliminate duplicate content issues and set RSS feeds correctly.
  • Enhanced Multilingual SEO: Updated Arabic URLs to be more descriptive, improving visibility.
  • Removed Test Pages: Deleted or redirected outdated and test pages to eliminate errors.
  • Revised Header Tags and Meta Descriptions: Ensured proper use of header tags and meta descriptions to enhance clarity and search engine understanding.
  • Updated Content: Replaced placeholder text with relevant, high-quality content.
  • Implemented Structured Data: Added structured data markup to help search engines better understand and present the site's content.
  • Submitted the site map.
  • Fixed robots.txt and submitted it to the root.
  • Added H1 titles for every page.
  • Added meta descriptions for every page.
  • Deleted irrelevant default meta descriptions (previously set in Yoast).
  • Disabled RSS feed pages to prevent duplication and missing canonical tags.
  • Worked on internal linking, especially between services pages and relevant content pages.
  • Conducted backlink acquisition.
  • Added structured data.
  • Set up an FAQ page.

SEO Results Achieved

Our efforts yielded remarkable results:

  • Increased Visibility: The site started ranking for high-value keywords, with a significant boost in organic traffic.
  • Improved User Experience: Enhanced site speed and usability led to lower bounce rates and higher engagement.
  • Enhanced Credibility: Cleaned backlink profile and professional content presentation improved the site's authority and trustworthiness.
  • Better Analytics Tracking: Accurate tracking of user behavior and site performance provided valuable insights for ongoing improvements.

Conclusion

This case study demonstrates the power of a well-executed SEO strategy in transforming a website's performance and visibility. By addressing critical issues with a targeted approach, we helped MMT achieve significant growth and establish itself as a trusted entity in the medical field.
